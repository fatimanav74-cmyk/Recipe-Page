Thai Noodles

Thai noodles are a vibrant and flavorful part of Thai cuisine, known for their perfect balance of sweet, sour, salty, and spicy tastes. Dishes like Pad Thai and Drunken Noodles combine rice noodles with fresh vegetables, herbs, and proteins, all tossed in rich, aromatic sauces. This recipe brings that same authentic street-food experience to your kitchen, using simple ingredients to create a delicious and satisfying meal that captures the essence of Thailand’s bold flavors.

Ingredients

  • 200g rice noodles
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 2 cloves garlic (chopped)
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrot, bell pepper, cabbage)
  • 1 cup chicken/shrimp/tofu (optional)
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon chili sauce
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 2 green onions (chopped)
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice

Instructions

  1. Boil rice noodles according to package instructions and set aside.
  2. Heat oil in a pan or wok over medium heat.
  3. Add garlic and sauté until fragrant.
  4. Add vegetables like bell peppers, carrots, and cabbage, and stir-fry.
  5. Add cooked chicken/shrimp/tofu (optional) and mix well.
  6. Add boiled noodles into the pan.
  7. Pour soy sauce, chili sauce, and a little sugar for flavor.
  8. Mix everything well and cook for 2–3 minutes.
  9. Garnish with green onions and lemon juice.
  10. Serve hot and enjoy your Thai noodles.
